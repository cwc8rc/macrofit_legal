MacroFit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

1. Data Ownership

At MacroFit, we believe your health and financial data is your own. We follow a "local-first" philosophy. Most sensitive data stays on your device.

2. Health Integration

MacroFit requests permission to read and write to Apple Health and Health Connect (Google Fit). We use this to adjust your calorie goals based on activity. We do not store this health data on our servers.

3. Social & Leaderboard

To power Social features, your username and points are synced to our secure database. This is the only data shared with other users in your leagues.
